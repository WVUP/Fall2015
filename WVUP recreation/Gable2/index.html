Board of Governors to meet Sept. 9

West Virginia University at Parkersburg Board of Governors will meet Wednesday, Sept. 9. The meeting will take place at 3:15 p.m. in the President’s conference room (room 1101). Complete agenda: September 9, 2015

Ensemble Espanol Spanish Dance Theatre kicks off WVU Parkersburg performance series Sept. 24

West Virginia University at Parkersburg’s Distinguished Performance Series presents Chicago-based dance company Ensemble Espanol Spanish Dance Theatre on Thursday, Sept. 24. The show begins at 7 p.m. in the College Activities Center.


WVU at Parkersburg Foundation welcomes new board members, says farewell to retirees

The WVU at Parkersburg Foundation recently welcomed five new board members and said farewell to eight retirees. Foundation Board members serve three year terms and are required to attend meetings and dedicate time and energy to the advancement of the Foundation and the college.
